Frequently asked questions about the platform and how to use it effectively.

How do I submit a complaint?

Register, log in, and open the complaint form from your dashboard or the complaints page.

How do I check progress?

Use your ticket ID on the tracking page or watch the notifications panel for updates.

Who handles assignment?

Administrators assign complaints to maintenance staff and can close tickets after resolution.
